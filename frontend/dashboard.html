Innovation Statement
🛡️
Officer-in-the-Loop
Zero auto-enforcement. Every challan requires explicit officer approval, making the system legally defensible and ethically sound.
⚡
Force Multiplier
1 officer monitors 50+ camera zones simultaneously. AI flags candidates; humans decide. 10x enforcement coverage with same manpower.
📊
Data-Driven Enforcement
Violation heatmaps guide patrol deployment. Post-event analytics drive zone policy. Every decision backed by evidence packets.
